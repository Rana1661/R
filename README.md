### **San Diego Budget Analysis (2013–2024)**

This project analyzes trends in the City of San Diego’s Operating and Capital budgets from 2013 to 2024 using Excel for data cleaning and Power BI for visualization. The goal is to evaluate financial performance, identify overspending and underspending patterns, and highlight the most costly capital projects.

##  **Project Objectives**

* Compare **Budgeted vs Actual** spending for Capital and Operating budgets.
* Identify **overspending and underspending** across fiscal years.
* Analyze spending by **fund types** and **departments**.
* Highlight the **most expensive capital projects** and year-over-year cost trends.
* Provide insights that support better financial planning.


## **Tools Used**

* **Excel** → Data cleaning, structuring, merging
* **Power BI** → cleaning, visuals, analytics



## **Datasets Included**

* actuals_capital_fy_datasd
* actuals_operating_datasd
* budget_capital_fy_datasd
* budget_operating_datasd
